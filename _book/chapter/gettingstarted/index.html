1.1 Introduction

Getting Started

Let’s start with a quick description of what to do after you unpack your brand new Pycom Device. The aim of this guide is not to bug you with complex details about it, but instead get you up and running as quickly as possible!

This chapter help you with the unboxing of and getting started with your device. If you come across any issues along the way, there is a Troubleshooting section that addresses the common problems. In case you can not find the answer to your issue, you can always ask questions in our Forum.
